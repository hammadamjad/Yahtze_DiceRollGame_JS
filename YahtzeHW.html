Welcome to Yahtzee Lite!

Briefly describe game/rules here. Tell the player what to do first.

  • Hit Play button to start the game.
  • Player have 10 rounds to roll the dice.
  • The straight 1,2,3,4,5 or 2,3,4,5,6 is the winner.
  • 'Keep' button will keep the specific dice and not change to the further rounds.



Describe the keepers buttons here
  • There are 5 keep buttons.
  • Whenever a keep button is pressed, the text changes to keeper.
  • The dice is kept till the end of the game and remaining are rolled.
